2007 Computer Science Contest Results

Thanks to the 25 students who participated in the seventeenth annual computer programming contest held Saturday, March 17, 2007. The contestants had about 3 hours to solve 8 problems. Everyone solved at least one. Results are shown below in order of ranking. In the case of ties, the penalty points were used to determine ranking.
