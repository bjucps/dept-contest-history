2011 BJU Programming Contest Results

On Saturday, March 19, 2011, 23 students participated in the Computer Science Programming Contest, a tradition since 1990. Contestants had about 3 hours to solve a series of problems with graduated difficulties.

Special thanks to Answers in Genesis, Gnoso, Inc., M-33 Integrated Solutions, and Worthwhile, Inc. for sponsoring the contest.
