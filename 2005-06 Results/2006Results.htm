2006 Computer Science Contest Results

Thanks to the 26 students who participated in the seventeenth annual computer programming contest held Saturday, March 18, 2006. The contestants had 3 hours to solve 8 problems. Everyone solved at least one. Results are shown below in order of ranking. In the case of ties, the penalty points were used to determine ranking.
